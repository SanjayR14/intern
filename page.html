THE PONNUSAMY ELITE

Authentic Chettinad Cuisine with a legacy of Excellence

Experience the rich culinary heritage of Chettinad at The Ponnusamy Elite. We bring you time-honoured recipes passed down through generations, prepared with the freshest ingredients and traditional techniques. Indulge in a symphony of flavors that define South India's most celebrated cuisine.

ABOUT THE BRAND

Ponnusamy has been synonymous with authentic South Indian, especially Chettinad, cuisine for decades. The Ponnusamy Elite is an extension of this legacy, offering an elevated dining experience while staying true to our roots. Our journey began with a passion for preserving traditional recipes and sharing the unique flavours of Chettinad with the world.

We meticulously source our spices and ingredients, ensuring quality and authenticity in every dish. Our chefs are masters of Chettinad cooking, bringing expertise and dedication to the kitchen. At Ponnusamy Elite, we invite you to savor the true taste of tradition in a refined and welcoming atmosphere.
